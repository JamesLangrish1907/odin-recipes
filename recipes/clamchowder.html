Clam Chowder

Description

This is my god awful clam chowder recipe. Eat at your own risk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of potatoes
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 3 cans of minced clams
  • 2 grated tomatoes
  • water to cover
  • 2 tbl spoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 stock cube

Steps

  1. Place potatoes, carrots, into a large skillet; pour in clam juice and add enough water to cover. Cook and stir over medium-low heat until vegetables are tender.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large, heavy saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour until smooth. Whisk in cream and stir constantly until thick and smooth. Stir in vegetable mixture with any juices until just heated through.
